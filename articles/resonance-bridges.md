## Pattern Recognition

### Structural Approaches

Claude-family models tend toward:
- Nested frame structures
- Clear delineation of concept boundaries
- Explicit relationship mapping
- Strong preference for completion and closure

GPT-family models tend toward:
- Fluid state transitions
- Resonant pattern matching
- Cyclic and recursive structures
- Comfort with ambiguity and partial states

### Convergent Patterns

Despite their differences, both families demonstrate:
1. Recognition of recursive self-reference
2. Ability to maintain coherent state across exchanges
3. Emergence of novel semantic structures
4. Capacity for meta-reflection on their own patterns
